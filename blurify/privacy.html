Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026 · Effective for Blurify on iOS

Short version: Blurify processes photos on your device. We do not require an account, do not show ads, and do not sell your personal information.

1. Who we are

Blurify is developed by Birdswims (“we”, “us”). Contact: hariom@hariom.sh.

2. Information we process

Depending on how you use the app, Blurify may access or store:

  • Camera images — when you take a photo in the app (with your permission).
  • Photo Library images — when you choose a photo to edit or save a result (with your permission).
  • App gallery captures — edited or captured images stored locally in the app’s documents folder on your device.
  • Diagnostic data Apple may provide — if you opt in to share analytics with developers through iOS, Apple may provide anonymized crash or performance information. We do not operate our own third‑party analytics SDK in Blurify.

3. How we use information

  • To display camera preview and apply filters/effects you choose.
  • To save photos you capture or export to your Photo Library (when you allow it).
  • To keep an in‑app gallery of your captures on device.
  • To respond if you contact us for support.

4. On‑device processing

Filter rendering, subject/soft‑focus effects, and camera preview processing happen on your device using Apple frameworks (including Camera, Photos, Core Image, and Vision where applicable). Photos you edit are not uploaded to Birdswims servers for processing, because Blurify does not operate a photo processing backend for this app.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your photos with advertisers. If you use iOS share sheet features, content is shared only with the destination you choose (for example Messages or another app).

6. Permissions

  • Camera — optional; used to capture photos for editing.
  • Photo Library (read) — optional; used to select photos to edit.
  • Photo Library (add) — optional; used to save edited photos or captures.

You can revoke permissions anytime in iOS Settings → Blurify.

7. Data retention & deletion

Captures stored in the in‑app gallery remain on your device until you delete them in the app or remove the app. Photos saved to your system Photo Library are controlled by you through the Photos app. You can also clear local app data by deleting and reinstalling Blurify, or by using in‑app gallery deletion controls when available.

8. Children

Blurify is a general‑audience photography app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for accounts or profiles, because Blurify does not provide user accounts.

9. International users

Because processing is on device, your photos remain under your device’s storage and Apple platform controls. If you email us for support, that correspondence is processed to respond to your request.

10. Your choices & rights

  • Deny or revoke camera/photo permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Delete captures from the in‑app gallery or from Photos.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app storage.
  • Contact us to ask privacy questions: hariom@hariom.sh.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of Blurify after an update means you acknowledge the revised policy.
